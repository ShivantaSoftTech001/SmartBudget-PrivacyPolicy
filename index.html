Last updated: January 15, 2026

Smart Budget Expense Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application.

1. Purpose of the App

Smart Budget Expense Tracker is designed only for calculation and tracking purposes. The app helps users record and calculate income and expense transactions.

  • This app is not a banking or payment application
  • It does not provide financial, investment, or legal advice
  • It does not offer loans, credit, or any financial services

2. Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, bank details, or payment information.

3. Expense & Transaction Data

All income, expense, and transaction data entered in the app:

  • Is stored locally on the user’s device
  • Is not uploaded to our servers
  • Is not accessed or monitored by us
  • Is not shared with any third party

We use Google Analytics for Firebase and Google Crashlytics to monitor app performance and stability.

These services may collect non-personal information such as:

This data is used only to improve app performance and user experience.

Google’s Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. The app does not access banking systems, payment networks, or store transaction data online. However, no digital system is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Smart Budget Expense Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
